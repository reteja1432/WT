Gabbar Singh

Review by : Teja

Genre: Action

Release Date: 18 August 2011

Rating: 4.8/5

Plot Summary:A police officer who embarks on a mission to take down a corrupt politician..

Pros:There is a scene in Gabbar Singh where Brahmanandam, a money lender, walks into the
villain's den and roars that his dues be settled.

Cons:The second half The hero is more powerful than villain, hence the hero vs villain thread is weak.

Overall:Inspired by a film character, Venkataratnam, a policeman, calls himself Gabbar Singh.!
